The HM3301 sensor platform allows you to use your HM3301 particulate matter sensor (more info) sensors with ESPHome.

The sensor communicate with board by IÂ²C protocol, and requires 3.3v.

# Example configuration entry
i2c:

sensor:
  - platform: hm3301
    pm_1_0:
      name: "PM1.0"
    pm_2_5:
      name: "PM2.5"
    pm_10_0:
      name: "PM10.0"
    aqi:
      name: "AQI"
      calculation_type: "CAQI"

Air Quality Sensor:Â¶

There is a sensor which calculates quality of air based on PM 2.5 and PM 10.0 values. There are two implementations:

  • AQI: USA air quality standard

  • CAQI: Europe air quality standard

sensor:
  - platform: hm3301
    pm_2_5:
      name: "PM2.5"
    pm_10_0:
      name: "PM10.0"
    aqi:
      name: "AQI"
      calculation_type: "CAQI"
